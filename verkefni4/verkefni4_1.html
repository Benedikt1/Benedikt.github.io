How many people wish to always live happily with their parents without trouble or worry? I think everyone would like that until they made their own family.

For me, I wasn't able to hold on to that fleeting dream.

My dad was a rich asshole who was nice in his youth but after some years
became an ass.

It was when I was eight that mom divorced but she lost the custody war and
now I can barely see her because dad doesn't permit it.

He got worse and after some time began to take his frustration out on me
whenever I was home.

To deal with the anger that built up I took up two completely different
things. First was martial arts to vent my anger before I reached home where
I needed to remain calm against him. The other is japanese anime, manga
and light novels. These helped distract my mind from him when I was at
home were, after making dinner for the asshole I would lock myself in my room.

I'm fifteen now and I've been getting ready for this day. Today is the same
day dad divorced with my mom a few years back and the hell began so I found
it appropriate to end my suffering on the same day.

I get home with an extra heavy backpack and quickly run up to my room and
lock the door just as I start to hear his ranting about food and money.

Ya, he makes me work like his personal slave so he can laze around. He
works the stock markets, and as much as I hate it, he is good at what he
does which is how he still has so much money after so long. Not like I get
access to much of it. Still, after he blows his extra money he uses me
to get more.
